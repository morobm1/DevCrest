Experience University of Washington Bothell at Residential Village

University of Washington Bothell is a vibrant, innovative campus located in the heart of the Pacific Northwest. Known for its commitment to student success, community engagement, and hands-on learning, UW Bothell offers a welcoming environment for conferences, summer programs, and retreats.

Residential Village is the newest living and learning community at UW Bothell, designed to provide modern accommodations and flexible event spaces for guests of all ages. Whether you're attending a university-sponsored event or hosting your own program, you'll enjoy:

  • State-of-the-art suites and apartments with private and shared options
  • Access to campus amenities, dining, and recreation
  • Beautiful natural surroundings and easy access to Seattle
  • Support from dedicated UW Bothell and Residential Village staff

Host your summer 2025 event at UWB Residential Village

Imagine hosting your summer program, conference, or retreat in a modern, vibrant living community right on the University of Washington Bothell campus. Whether you are planning a youth camp, academic seminar, leadership retreat, or professional gathering, our spaces are designed to make your event seamless and memorable.

Why choose Residential Village?

The UWB Residential Village offers a variety of flexible spaces, modern amenities, and personalized support to ensure your event is a success. Our experienced team will work with you every step of the way — from planning to execution — to make sure your guests have an unforgettable experience on campus.
