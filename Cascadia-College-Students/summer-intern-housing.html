Horizon Hall

Horizon Hall offers spacious, fully furnished apartments ideal for students participating in summer internships in the local area. Each unit includes in-unit laundry, high-speed internet, and a modern kitchen setup. Interns will enjoy a vibrant and inclusive community, convenient access to campus and city amenities, and flexible lease terms designed specifically for summer stays.

Please note: Residential Village summer housing is designated for individuals who are actively participating in a local internship program. Proof of internship, such as an offer letter or documentation from your employer, will be required as part of the application process.
