UNIVERSITY OF UTAH STUDENTS

Ivory University House is a vibrant and inclusive community that upholds high standards for all residents. We understand that a healthy and focused environment plays a crucial role in academic success and personal growth. That’s why we have intentionally fostered a substance-free culture within our residence halls and for safety purposes, we do not welcome overnight guests. Amenities such as recital and practice space, yoga, meditation, fitness programs, game rooms, art studio, mentors in intellectual, physical, spiritual, and psychological areas of study and exploration, cooking classes, and outdoor activities are all part of the Ivory University House experience. Join us as we work together to create an empowering environment for you and all of our residents.
