Conference Dining & Catering

Enjoy delicious, convenient dining at the Terrace Dining Pavilion during your stay at UW Bothell Residential Village.
Our dining team is committed to providing fresh, local, and sustainable meals for all conference guests.

Terrace Dining Pavilion
  • Location: Central campus, steps from Residential Village
  • Service Style: All-you-care-to-eat buffet
  • Meal Times: Breakfast, Lunch, and Dinner available daily
  • Dietary Needs: Vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and allergen-friendly options
  • Group Dining: Reserved seating and group meal plans for conference guests
  • Hours & Menus: View current hours and menus
What's Inside the Terrace Dining Pavilion?

Clean Plate

Allergen-friendly station serving delicious meals free from the top 9 allergens and gluten. Enjoy safe, wholesome options for every guest.

Urban Kitchen + Market Bar

Classic and contemporary entrées, global cuisine, and a fresh market bar with salads, fruits, and more. Rotating chef-inspired menus daily.

The Drop

Beverage bar featuring espresso drinks, coffee, tea, and refreshing cold beverages. Perfect for a morning boost or afternoon break.

The Food Lab

Creative, chef-driven pop-up concepts and themed menus. Experience something new each week with innovative flavors and fun presentations.
