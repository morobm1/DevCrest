Welcome to Modern Living
Ferry Street Flats offers fully furnished studio and studio loft apartments designed for traveling professionals, essential workers, and members of Eugene’s workforce. Each unit is move-in ready, combining comfort, convenience, and functionality for short- or long-term stays. Located near downtown Eugene, major hospitals, business districts, and the University of Oregon, Ferry Street Flats provides easy access to work and daily essentials while maintaining a quiet, professional atmosphere. With flexible lease options, modern furnishings, and a focus on simplicity, Ferry Street Flats serves as a dependable home base for those who need to stay productive and feel at home while on assignment or in transition.

Prime Location

Live where convenience meets connection. Ferry Street Flats puts you within walking distance of downtown Eugene’s restaurants, cafes, and nightlife, making it an ideal home for those working in the service and hospitality industry. Quick access to local hospitals, the University of Oregon, and public transit makes commuting simple. We are proud to have hosted numerous travel nurses and medical professionals who value comfort, flexibility, and location. Plus, with the riverfront trail system just steps away, it’s easy to unwind in nature after a busy day.

Great Amenities

Ferry Street Flats is thoughtfully equipped to support your lifestyle. Enjoy the convenience of a secure building, dedicated bike storage, secure package pickup, and on-site parking. Each floor features a shared community kitchen and laundry room, offering added flexibility for daily living. Relax or connect with others on the rooftop deck, and feel right at home with our pet-friendly policy. Every amenity is designed to make your stay comfortable, safe, and convenient.
