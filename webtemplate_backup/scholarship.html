About Our Scholarships

At Ivory University House, we believe in fostering opportunities for our residents to thrive academically and financially. Our team is dedicated to connecting students with scholarship and work opportunities, to help ensure that they have the resources to pursue their dream without financial constraints.

  • Merit-based and need-based scholarships available
  • Open to current and incoming residents
  • Simple application process
  • Opportunities for renewal each year
